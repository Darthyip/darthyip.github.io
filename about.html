About Me

My name is Andrea and I am 41 years old. I live with my partner Mark in Dallas, Oregon with our 9-year-old pug Eowyn (pronounces AA-OH-WIN), who is also a service dog, and our 9-year-old Siamese/Maine coon mix cat named Shadow Fax (Fax for short). Yes, some of you fellow The Lord of the Rings geeks will know where those names came from.

I graduated from Oregon School of Massage and was licensed in 2016, creating The Dragon and the Phoenix Healing Arts, LLC in June of 2017. I also went to school to become a Vet Tech (a nurse for animals) in 2005 and worked for 10 years as a tech.

I am a disabled veteran who served in the Navy. I was hurt and had to be discharged. I found massage and Reiki to be one of the only things that helped my chronic pain. I wanted to give that same hope and healing to others. So that is why I work with both people and pets. Dogs, cats, horses and people all deserve healing and to live pain free. All veterans, first responders and service animals get discounts here. To give my appreciation for all you give to us.

I look forward to talking with you and to set up an appointment so we can get you and your pets where you need to be on your journey to optimal health and happiness, until then... Andrea Cirrito LMT lic # 22412.
